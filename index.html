WHITE CHOCOLATE OREO TRUFFLES

Quick and easy no-bake white chocolate oreo truffles for any occasion! ☺ (Recipe by Pretty, Simple, Sweet.)

Addictive and fudge-y oreo truffles that are coated with white chocolate. The best part? They're made with only a few ingredients- and you won’t be able to get enough of them!

You're going to need these ingredients:

  • One regular package of Oreo cookies (with filling)
  • 8 oz cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 16 oz white chocolate, melted
  • OPTIONAL: 6oz milk chocolate, melted, for garnishing

How to prepare the truffles:

  1. Crush Oreo cookies in a plastic bag until finely ground using a rolling pin.
  2. Reserve 2 tablespoons of cookie crumbs for garnishing.
  3. In a medium bowl, mix cream cheese and cookie crumbs until well combined.
  4. Refrigerate mixture for about an hour, then shape into 1-inch/2.5-cm balls and place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  5. Freeze until firm, about one hour (freezing will make the balls firm and easier to dip into the chocolate).
  6. Using two forks, dip and coat balls with melted white chocolate and place on parchment paper.
  7. Drizzle with milk chocolate, if desired. Sprinkle with reserved cookie crumbs. Place in the fridge for 10 minutes, until chocolate hardens.
  8. Keep in the refrigerator and serve chilled, so the chocolate won’t melt. Truffles will keep for up to 4 days in an airtight container.
